Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements for BTech Level 8?

Admission requires completion of Level 7 or equivalent, submission of academic transcripts, a personal statement, and an interview. International students must provide proof of English proficiency.

How long is the BTech Level 8 program?

The program is typically 2 years (4 semesters) of full-time study. Part-time options are available and can extend the duration to 3-4 years depending on course load.

What career opportunities are available after graduation?

Graduates pursue careers as Software Engineers, Data Scientists, Cloud Architects, Cybersecurity Analysts, AI/ML Engineers, IoT Specialists, and Technology Consultants. Our placement rate is 95% within 6 months of graduation.

Are there scholarship opportunities available?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships, need-based financial aid, and industry-sponsored scholarships. Contact our admissions office for current scholarship opportunities and application deadlines.

Can I visit the campus before applying?

Absolutely! We offer campus tours every Wednesday at 10 AM and 2 PM. You can also schedule a virtual tour or attend our open house events. Contact us to arrange your visit.
